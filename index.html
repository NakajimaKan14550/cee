Thermal Intelligence in the AI Era

A (Chronix): With the rise of high-density AI servers, traditional temperature sensors—which only measure a single point—are no longer enough. We are seeing 'micro-hotspots' that appear and disappear in seconds. Our clients need a way to monitor the thermal distribution across the entire rack in real-time.
B (Calumino): That is exactly why our privacy-protected thermal sensors are gaining traction. Unlike standard high-resolution thermography, our technology detects anomalies without identifying individual faces. We use a bolometer-based array that captures the 'thermal footprint' of the airflow.
A: "Integration is key. We want to use the thermal data to trigger an immediate increase in fan speed or to shift workloads before a component fails. However, the environment is electrically noisy. How do your sensors handle Electromagnetic Interference (EMI), and what is the refresh rate? We need to capture those transient heat spikes before they lead to a thermal shutdown."

テーマ：民生品（COTS）のジレンマ

ZES: Radiation Protection for COTS

A (Chronix): Our clients in the 'New Space' sector are struggling with a dilemma. They want the high performance and low cost of COTS components, but they can't afford a system crash due to SEE (Single Event Effects) in high-radiation orbits. How exactly does your technology allow standard chips to survive in such a harsh environment?
B (ZES): It’s about a hybrid approach. We don't just rely on shielding. Our ZES technology integrates physical-layer protection with intelligent latch-up detection and recovery. We monitor the current in real-time. If we detect a radiation-induced surge, our system reacts in nanoseconds to prevent permanent damage, while maintaining data integrity through TMR (Triple Modular Redundancy).
A: That sounds promising. But in a high-speed processing mission, even a few milliseconds of downtime can be critical. If a radiation event triggers your latch-up protection, how fast is the recovery time? And how do you ensure that the system state is restored to exactly where it was before the surge?

Case 1: Thermal Intelligence in the AI Era

（AI時代の熱インテリジェンス）

A (クロニクス): 高密度AIサーバの台頭により、一点のみを計測する従来の温度センサでは不十分になっています。秒単位で発生・消失する「マイクロ・ホットスポット」が見られます。クライアントはラック全体の温度分布を監視する方法を必要としていますが、プライバシーも非常に懸念しています。

B (Calumino): だからこそ弊社のプライバシー保護型熱センサが注目されています。個人の顔を特定することなく熱異常を検知でき、気流の「熱フットプリント」を捉えるボロメータ配列を使用しています。

A: 統合が鍵です。熱データを使用してファンの回転数を上げたり、故障前にワークロードを移動させたい。電磁妨害（EMI）対策はどうなっていますか？シャットダウン前に一時的な熱スパイクを捉える必要があります。
