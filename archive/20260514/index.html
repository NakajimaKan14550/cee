FPGA SiP and Evolvable Hardware

English Script

A (Chronix): For the Mars mission, we’re proposing the FPGA SiP (System-in-Package) integrated with 3D stacking technology.

B: Why not use a standard radiation-hardened ASIC? It’s usually more power-efficient.

A: True, but in deep space, physical maintenance is impossible. With FPGA, we can remotely reconfigure the hardware logic to adapt to unexpected sensor failures or new mission requirements.

B: I see. So the 3D integration allows you to pack high-density processing into a tiny, radiation-shielded footprint?

A: Exactly. It’s about creating "evolvable hardware" that can survive and optimize itself in the most extreme environments.

A: 火星探査ミッションに向けて、3D積層技術を統合したFPGA SiPを提案しています。

B: 標準的な放射線耐性ASICを使わないのはなぜですか？

A: 深宇宙では物理メンテナンスが不可能です。FPGAなら遠隔で回路を再構成でき、予期せぬ故障にも対応できます。

💡 重要単語: Diffraction limit (回折限界), High-fidelity (高忠実度), Robustness (堅牢性)
